# Refund Policy

**Last updated: February 16, 2026**

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with Triggerfishh, we offer a **full refund within 30 days** of your purchase—no questions asked.

## How to Request a Refund

1. **Email us:** [contact@triggerfishh.com](mailto:contact@triggerfishh.com)
2. **Include in your email:**
   - The email address used for your purchase
   - Your order receipt or transaction ID
   - A brief description of why you'd like a refund (optional, but helps us improve)
3. **We'll respond:** Within 2-3 business days with approval and next steps

## Refund Processing

- **Timeline:** Refunds are processed immediately upon approval
- **How you receive it:** Refunded to your original payment method
- **Bank processing time:** 5-10 business days (depends on your financial institution)
- **Amount:** Full purchase price
- **Note:** Payment processing fees vary by bank and region

## License Key & Access

Once you receive your license key and download Triggerfishh:
- Your license key is yours to use
- If refunded, the license key remains valid for **30 days after the refund date**, then expires
- After the refund window closes, your license will no longer be active

## Outside the 30-Day Window

Refund requests made after 30 days may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We reserve the right to decline refund requests that fall outside our standard policy.
